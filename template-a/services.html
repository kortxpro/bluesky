Our Services

Comprehensive commercial cleaning and facility maintenance solutions.

Every facility has its own demands — different surfaces, different schedules, different standards. We build each scope of work from scratch based on your building, your traffic patterns, and what "clean" actually needs to look like for your operation. Below is a breakdown of our core capabilities and what each engagement includes.

Core Service

Janitorial Services

Our janitorial programs are built around your schedule, not ours. Whether you need nightly service for a 200,000-square-foot office campus or twice-weekly attention for a smaller retail location, we design the scope to match. Every account gets a dedicated supervisor, written task lists, and regular quality audits so nothing slips through the cracks.

  • Custom frequency plans — daily, weekly, or rotating schedules
  • Office, retail, medical, and industrial facility coverage
  • Scope planning walkthrough before contract start
  • Task-level checklists reviewed by on-site supervisors
  • Restroom sanitation, trash removal, and common area upkeep
  • Monthly quality audit reports with photographic documentation

Exterior Maintenance

Pressure Washing

Weather is relentless — humidity, rain, and organic growth take a visible toll on building exteriors fast. Our pressure washing crews run commercial-grade equipment rated for high-volume output, and we adjust PSI and nozzle configuration based on the surface material. Concrete, brick, stucco, painted surfaces — each one gets handled differently because they have to be.

  • Building facades, entryways, and canopies
  • Sidewalks, walkways, and parking structures
  • Loading docks and dumpster pads
  • Surface-specific PSI and detergent selection
  • Scheduled maintenance programs or one-time service
  • Before-and-after photo documentation

Specialty Cleaning

Window Cleaning

Dirty glass is one of the first things people notice about a building, and one of the last things maintenance teams get around to. We handle interior and exterior commercial glass with professional-grade squeegee systems, water-fed poles for upper floors, and the safety protocols required for elevated work. The result is streak-free glass that actually stays clean longer because we treat frames and sills at the same time.

  • Interior and exterior glass surfaces
  • Frame, track, and sill cleaning included
  • Water-fed pole systems for multi-story buildings
  • Full compliance with fall protection and safety standards
  • Streak-free finish with professional-grade equipment
  • Recurring schedules or seasonal one-time service

Deep Cleaning

Carpet Cleaning

Surface vacuuming only does so much. Over time, soil works its way into carpet backing where it breaks down fibers, holds odors, and triggers allergies. Our hot water extraction process reaches the base of the carpet pile to pull out what regular cleaning leaves behind. We pre-treat high-traffic lanes and stains individually before running extraction equipment across the full area.

  • Hot water extraction reaching carpet backing
  • Pre-treatment for stains, high-traffic zones, and spots
  • Soil, allergen, and odor removal at the fiber level
  • Fast-dry process to minimize downtime
  • Furniture moving and replacement as needed
  • Post-cleaning inspection and walkthrough with client
Request a carpet cleaning quote →
Hard Floor Care

Floor Stripping & Waxing

Hard floors take a beating in commercial environments — foot traffic, rolling carts, spills, and cleaning chemicals all wear through finish coats faster than most people realize. Our floor care process starts with a full chemical strip to remove old wax and buildup, followed by surface prep, sealant application, multiple coats of commercial-grade wax, and a high-speed buff to bring back the shine. We work with VCT, ceramic tile, concrete, and terrazzo.

  • Full chemical strip of old wax and finish buildup
  • Surface prep, cleaning, and neutralization
  • Sealant coat followed by multi-layer wax application
  • High-speed burnishing for a durable, high-gloss finish
  • VCT, ceramic tile, polished concrete, and terrazzo surfaces
  • Overnight and weekend scheduling to avoid business disruption
Request a floor care quote →
Project-Based

Post-Construction Cleanup

Construction dust gets into everything — HVAC systems, light fixtures, window tracks, behind outlet covers. A standard cleaning crew is not equipped to handle the scope of what a build-out or renovation leaves behind. Our post-construction teams work in phases: rough clean to haul debris and scrape surfaces, then detail clean to address every fixture, surface, and corner before the space is ready for occupancy.

  • Debris removal, dumpster coordination, and haul-out
  • Construction dust removal from all surfaces and systems
  • Window, glass, and frame cleaning including label and adhesive removal
  • Hard floor scrub, seal, and polish
  • Light fixture, vent, and diffuser cleaning
  • Final detail pass and punch-list walkthrough with GC
