About BlueSky General Services

Built on a commitment to doing the job right. Over a decade later, that same commitment drives every facility we maintain.

Our Story

From one van to 500+ clients.

BlueSky General Services was founded more than ten years ago with a straightforward idea: commercial cleaning should be measurable, not just promised. It started with a single van, a small crew, and a handful of office accounts. There was no outside funding and no shortcuts — just consistent work and honest reporting.

That approach turned out to be exactly what property managers and facility directors were looking for. Word spread. Contracts grew. Today BlueSky serves over 500 active commercial accounts, covering everything from nightly janitorial to post-construction cleanup. The company is bigger, but the operating principle hasn't changed: document the work, measure the results, and let the numbers speak for themselves.

Our Approach

KPI-driven performance is not a marketing line. It is how we operate.

Every BlueSky contract starts with defined quality benchmarks — not vague promises, but specific measurable targets tied to scope, frequency, and expected outcomes. We track inspection scores, crew completion rates, issue resolution times, and client satisfaction responses on a rolling basis.

Monthly reports go directly to our clients with photo-documented inspections, open ticket logs, and trend data. If something is off, we see it in the numbers before it becomes a complaint. This is not a dashboard we built to impress prospects. It is the operational framework every account manager uses daily to run their facilities.

Sustainability

Green-certified products and methods across every job site.

Every cleaning chemical we use is third-party certified for indoor air quality and environmental safety. We don't stock conventional products as a backup — the green-certified line is the only line. This was a deliberate decision we made years ago, and it applies to every facility we service regardless of contract size.

Beyond product selection, our teams follow eco-friendly application methods that reduce water waste and chemical usage without compromising cleaning outcomes. We hold current certifications for sustainable facility maintenance and participate in ongoing training to stay ahead of evolving standards. For tenants and building occupants, that means cleaner air and a healthier workspace. For property managers, it means one less compliance concern.

Our People

Background-checked, trained, and held to documented standards.

Every crew member at BlueSky goes through a background screening and a structured onboarding process before they step foot on a client site. We do not hire and hope for the best — we train to specific protocols that cover everything from chemical handling and equipment use to client communication and emergency procedures.

Once on the job, team members follow documented task lists for each facility. Supervisors conduct regular inspections and provide feedback tied to the same KPIs our clients see in their monthly reports. When standards are met consistently, our people know it. When something needs correcting, they know that too. It is a straightforward system, and it works because everyone is on the same page.
