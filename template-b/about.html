About BlueSky

About BlueSky General Services

A cleaning company built by someone who has been on the crew, managed the crew, and knows what it takes to keep a building right.

Our Story

BlueSky did not start in a boardroom. It started with a mop.

Before BlueSky existed, our founder was a one-man cleaning crew. He worked nights in office buildings, learned every floor type by scrubbing them himself, and earned his first contracts by being the most reliable person in the room.

That was over ten years ago. Today, BlueSky General Services serves hundreds of commercial clients, with dedicated crews serving commercial offices, retail centers, construction sites, and everything in between. But the core principle has not changed: show up on time, do the work right, and do not make excuses.

Every decision we make -- from the products we use to the people we hire -- goes through a simple filter: would this be good enough for the first client who trusted us?

Our Approach

Metrics, not opinions. That is how we hold ourselves accountable.

We do not ask you to trust that the building is clean. We prove it. Every BlueSky engagement is tracked through a structured quality assurance system that generates real data -- not vague status updates.

  • GPS-verified clock-in and clock-out on every shift
  • Photo documentation of completed work areas
  • Monthly QA scores with room-level detail
  • 24-hour response window for all service issues
  • Quarterly business reviews with trend analysis

What this means in practice

You get a login. You see when our crew arrived, what they completed, and photographic proof. If something does not meet the standard, we know about it before you do -- and we fix it within a day.

Sustainability

We clean buildings without making the air worse inside them.

Most commercial cleaning chemicals are effective but harsh. They leave residues that irritate airways, damage finishes over time, and create problems that are invisible until they are not.

BlueSky uses EPA-registered, low-VOC products across every service line. Our floor chemicals are Green Seal certified. Our restroom products are fragrance-free and biodegradable. We chose these products because they work just as well as the harsh alternatives -- and they do not create new problems while solving old ones.

  • EPA-registered, low-VOC cleaning solutions across all services
  • Green Seal certified floor care products
  • HEPA-filtered vacuums on all janitorial routes
  • Microfiber systems that reduce chemical and water waste
  • Quarterly product audits to ensure compliance

Our People

Trained, background-checked, and assigned to your building.

The most common complaint about cleaning companies is inconsistency. Different people show up every night, nobody knows the building, and quality varies wildly. We solved this by doing the obvious thing: assigning dedicated crews to each facility.

Your crew learns your building. They know which conference rooms get used hardest. They know the floor type in the lobby, the soap dispensers in the third-floor restroom, and the spots that need extra attention on Mondays after the weekend.

  • Background checks and drug screening for every hire
  • 40+ hour onboarding program covering safety and technique
  • Dedicated crew assignments -- the same faces every visit
  • Site-specific training before the first shift at your facility
  • Ongoing skill certifications for specialty services
