Core Service

Janitorial Services

Your lobby, your restrooms, your break rooms, your offices. These spaces get used hard every day, and they need a crew that treats them with the same attention at 11 PM that you would expect at 9 AM.

We build custom janitorial programs around your facility's layout, traffic patterns, and schedule. Daily, weekly, and monthly tasks are mapped to specific crew members and tracked through our QA platform so nothing falls through the cracks.

  • Break room and kitchen deep cleaning
  • Elevator and stairwell maintenance
  • Conference room reset and sanitization
  • Periodic deep cleaning of high-touch surfaces
  • Custom task scheduling by zone

Exterior Care

Pressure Washing

First impressions start at the curb. Walkways, storefronts, parking garages, dumpster pads, and building exteriors collect grime faster than most people realize. Our pressure washing crews use commercial-grade equipment calibrated to the surface -- because the PSI that cleans concrete will damage stucco.

We handle one-time jobs and recurring schedules. Most commercial properties benefit from quarterly exterior washing, and we build that into your scope so the work happens without you having to think about it.

What's Included
  • Storefront and entrance cleaning
  • Sidewalk and walkway degreasing
  • Parking garage and structure washing
  • Dumpster pad and loading dock cleaning
  • Building exterior surface washing
  • Graffiti and stain removal
  • Drive-through lane cleaning
  • Surface-specific PSI calibration

Glass Care

Window Cleaning

Clean windows are one of those things nobody notices until they are dirty. Interior glass, exterior panes, entrance doors, partitions -- all of it affects how a building looks and feels. Our crews handle single-story retail and multi-story commercial buildings using a combination of traditional squeegee work and water-fed pole systems.

We schedule window cleaning around your building's needs, typically monthly for interior glass and quarterly for exterior, but we adjust based on your environment and preferences.

What's Included
  • Interior window and glass cleaning
  • Exterior window cleaning up to 4 stories
  • Entrance door and sidelight glass
  • Interior glass partitions and dividers
  • Frame and sill wipe-down
  • Hard water stain removal
  • Skylight and atrium glass (by request)
  • Post-construction window detail

Soft Floors

Carpet Cleaning

Commercial carpet takes a beating. Foot traffic, spills, and daily wear break down fibers and trap dirt that vacuuming alone cannot remove. Our carpet cleaning uses hot water extraction -- the method recommended by every major carpet manufacturer -- to pull soil from deep in the pile and restore texture and appearance.

For facilities that cannot handle wet carpet downtime, we also offer low-moisture encapsulation cleaning. The carpet is walkable within an hour, making it ideal for offices, retail spaces, and medical facilities that operate during normal business hours.

What's Included
  • Hot water extraction (steam cleaning)
  • Low-moisture encapsulation
  • Pre-treatment of high-traffic lanes
  • Spot and stain removal
  • Carpet protectant application
  • Furniture moving and replacement
  • Deodorizing treatment
  • Color-safe cleaning solutions
Request Carpet Cleaning Quote

Hard Floors

Floor Stripping & Waxing

VCT, LVT, terrazzo, concrete, and natural stone all have different maintenance needs, but they share one thing: a quality floor finish makes the entire space look sharper. Our floor crews strip old wax down to bare tile, repair any damage, and apply multiple coats of commercial-grade finish that protects the surface and delivers a consistent gloss.

We also handle ongoing maintenance -- burnishing, scrub-and-recoat, and auto-scrubbing -- to extend the life of each wax application and keep your floors looking right between full strip cycles.

What's Included
  • Chemical stripping of old finish
  • Tile repair and baseboard cleaning
  • Multi-coat wax application
  • High-speed burnishing
  • Scrub-and-recoat maintenance
  • Terrazzo and stone polishing
  • Non-slip treatment options
  • Custom finish gloss levels

Construction

Post-Construction Cleanup

Construction crews build. We clean up after them. Post-construction cleanup is a specialized service that requires a team who understands the phases of a build and what "clean" means when a certificate of occupancy is on the line.

We work in three phases: rough clean during active construction to keep the site manageable, final clean after trades are finished but before punch list, and touch-up clean before the owner or tenant takes possession. Each phase has a different scope, different tools, and different standards.

What's Included
  • Phase 1: Rough clean (debris, dust, bulk removal)
  • Phase 2: Final clean (surfaces, fixtures, glass)
  • Phase 3: Touch-up and punch list clean
  • Construction dust and drywall compound removal
  • Window label and sticker removal
  • Fixture and hardware polishing
  • HVAC vent and register cleaning
  • Final inspection preparation
