What We Do

How can we help?

Three ways to understand what BlueSky brings to your facility.

Janitorial Programs

Recurring cleaning for offices, lobbies, restrooms, and common areas. We set the schedule, train the crew, and handle QA so you do not have to.

Exterior & Surface Care

Pressure washing, window cleaning, and facade maintenance. We handle the stuff that shapes how people see your building from the outside.

Floor & Specialty

Carpet extraction, VCT stripping, wax application, and post-construction detail cleaning. The work that turns a space from functional to polished.

Commercial Office

Multi-tenant office buildings, corporate campuses, and co-working spaces. We handle everything from nightly trash pulls to quarterly deep cleans.

Retail & Hospitality

Storefronts, shopping centers, hotels, and restaurants. High-traffic spaces that demand a crew that understands foot-traffic patterns and brand standards.

Construction & Development

General contractors and developers who need reliable post-construction cleanup. We work on your schedule, not ours, and we meet final inspection requirements.

Fully Licensed & Insured

Accountable Scheduling

GPS clock-in, real-time job tracking, and photo documentation on every visit. You know exactly when we arrived, what we did, and when we left.

Dedicated Crews
