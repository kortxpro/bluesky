About

Built on a simple belief: commercial cleaning companies could do better.

BlueSky General Services was founded with a straightforward idea: commercial cleaning should be consistent, measurable, and honest. No inflated promises. No corners cut. Just reliable work, documented and delivered on schedule.

What began as a small operation serving office buildings has grown into a company serving over 500 commercial accounts. Along the way, we added pressure washing, window cleaning, carpet care, floor maintenance, and post-construction cleanup to our services.

Our growth came from repeat clients and referrals, not from marketing campaigns. We built our reputation one facility at a time, and we continue to operate that way. Every job gets a quality inspection. Every client gets a direct point of contact. That has not changed since the beginning.

How we work.

01

Assessment first

We walk your facility before quoting. Every space is different, and we build service plans around what you actually need, not a generic package.

02

Assigned teams

You get a dedicated crew who knows your building. Consistency matters. The same people, the same standards, every visit.

03

Quality inspections

Every job is inspected and documented. If something is missed, we fix it before you have to ask. Our reporting system keeps both sides accountable.

04

Direct communication

No call centers. No ticket systems. You reach the person responsible for your account. Problems get solved in hours, not days.

Sustainability

Cleaner buildings, lighter footprint.

We use EPA-certified cleaning products, microfiber systems that reduce water and chemical waste, and HEPA-filtered equipment across all our operations. Our teams are trained in green cleaning protocols, and we actively track our chemical and water usage to reduce environmental impact year over year. It is not a marketing line. It is how we operate.
